## Objection Handling

**"This sounds too good to be true"**
> "I understand. That's why we measure everything independently. We don't charge based on promises - only actual time saved, timestamped and documented. You can audit the numbers anytime."

**"We've tried automation before and it didn't work"**
> "That's actually common - many automation projects fail because they don't have buy-in or proper measurement. YIELD Frameworkâ„¢ is different because (1) we measure the baseline first, (2) we measure results in parallel before going live, (3) you only pay for actual results. If it doesn't work, you don't pay."

**"We're too busy to do the baseline measurement"**
> "That's exactly why we're doing this. The baseline measurement IS the data you need. During those two weeks, you're logging time anyway - we're just asking you to be precise about it. It actually takes less time than usual because you're paying attention."

**"Can't you just automate everything?"**
> "Not everything should be automated. Some decisions are best made by humans. Our goal is to automate the repetitive, manual, low-value work so your team can focus on what only you can do - program development, client relationships, strategy."
